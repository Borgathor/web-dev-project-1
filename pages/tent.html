Midden Hold

The Adventure Begins

You head towards the tent and take stock of the contents of the magicians cart. Mostly generic traveling gear with the occasional item you can't identify, presumably tools of the trade. One item in particular catches your eye: a gem encrusted scroll case. Mesmerised you reach out to touch it and feel an incredible warmth as the item glows bright white before releasing the energy over your shoulder hitting a nearby roof which immediately catches alight. Knowing all eyes will quickly be on you, you have a split second to make a decision.

Dash Into The Tent before Anyone Spots You Make a Run For It
